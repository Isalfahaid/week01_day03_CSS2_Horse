Horse

Horses are hoofed mammals that have lived eith humans for thousands of years. Almost all of the horses alive today are domesticated and descend from extinct wild horses . Horses have roamed the planet for about 50 million years.

Horses are adapted to run . allowing them to quickly escape predators. possessing an excellent sense of balance and a strong fight-or-flight response.
